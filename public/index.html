ABOUT THE FOUNDER

The story behind OptionsLab and why it was built.

AB

Nifty F&O Quant Dev 🇮🇳 Built in India

"I was trading Nifty F&O every week and getting blindsided on expiry day. Every tool gave me price data. None showed me where the money was actually sitting."

So I built what I wished existed — a platform that decodes FII positioning, GEX walls, and dark pool signals before the move happens. Not after.

Mission
LEVEL THE PLAYING FIELD FOR INDIAN F&O TRADERS
Institutions have had GEX data, dark pool signals, and FII flow analysis for years. Retail traders deserve the same tools.

The Story

After years of trading Nifty F&O weekly, I noticed a consistent pattern: retail traders kept getting caught on the wrong side of big moves. Not because our technical analysis was wrong, but because we had no visibility into what institutional traders were actually doing. We were reacting to price. They were positioning before the move.

I started reverse-engineering how FII net futures positioning, Open Interest walls, and Gamma Exposure interact to create the stop hunts and expiry pins that retail traders experience every Thursday. OptionsLab is that research, turned into a platform you can use every trading day.

We don't sell your data. Your broker credentials never leave your device.

Summary: We collect your email and mobile to create your account. Your Angel One credentials are stored locally on your device only. We do not sell your data to anyone. Ever.

1. Who we are

OptionsLab (optionslab.in) is an options analytics platform for Indian F&O traders. We are NOT SEBI registered. Contact: support@optionslab.in

2. Information we collect

Account info: Name, mobile number, email. Your Angel One Client ID is stored to identify your account — but your MPIN, SmartAPI key, and TOTP secret are stored locally on your device only and never transmitted to our servers.

Usage data: Anonymised usage metrics (which features you use, session duration) to improve the platform. Not linked to your personal identity.

We never: sell your data to third parties; share with advertisers; access your broker account or trading history; store your broker credentials on our servers; send unsolicited marketing without consent.

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Passwords and MPINs are hashed using bcrypt. Payment processing is handled by Razorpay — we do not store card details.

5. Your rights

You can access, correct, or delete your account data at any time. Email support@optionslab.in to exercise these rights.

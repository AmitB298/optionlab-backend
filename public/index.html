FAQs

Common Questions

Is Jobber Pro really free to start? +
Yes. Download and use Jobber Pro completely free with the full options chain, basic Greeks, PCR, Max Pain, and the entire Academy. No credit card, no trial period, no expiry. Upgrade to Pro or Elite only when you need the advanced analytics and smart money features.
Where does the market data come from? +
Jobber Pro connects to NSE via Angel One's SmartAPI. You connect your own Angel One broker account — we don't proxy or store any market data. The data flows directly from the exchange to your desktop at 2.2-second refresh intervals during market hours.
Is my broker login safe? +
Your Angel One credentials are encrypted and stored only on your device. We store only your Client ID in our database for licence validation — never your password, API key, or TOTP secret. We can't access your trading account even if we wanted to.
Does Jobber Pro work on Mac or Linux? +
Currently Jobber Pro is Windows-only (Windows 10/11, 64-bit). Mac and Linux versions are planned for a future release. The web dashboard (this site) works on all browsers and devices.
What is your refund policy? +
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied within 7 days of your first paid subscription, contact us and we'll issue a full refund — no questions asked. After 7 days, refunds are not available for the current billing period.
Do I need programming knowledge to use the advanced analytics? +
No. All analytics — including GARCH volatility modeling, VPIN, and Kyle's Lambda — are presented as visual dashboards with clear signals. You don't need to understand the math. The Academy section explains what each metric means and how to use it in your trading decisions.
